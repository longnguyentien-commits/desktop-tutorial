# 00. Game Overview

## Concept

**Dragon vs Monsters: Hatchery Defense** is a lane-based tactical defense game. The player builds a dragon formation on a grid to stop monsters from crossing the dragon side and breaking through the castle wall. The game combines Plants vs. Zombies-style lane defense with Teamfight Tactics-style shop buying, bench management, XP leveling, and 3-copy merging.

Dragons are bought from a 5-slot shop. The player earns Gold by killing monsters during waves, then uses Gold to buy dragons, reroll the shop, and buy XP. Dragon visuals now use only the **Asset DML** folder.

## Pillars

1. **Readable lane defense, strategic economy**  
   Monsters advance from right to left across 5 lanes. The player buys, places, and merges dragons while managing Gold, bench space, XP timing, and board cap.

2. **TFT-style dragon drafting**  
   The shop offers 5 random dragons. Each dragon has element traits and a cost from 1 Gold to 5 Gold. Rerolling and leveling shape the player's composition.

3. **Element identity**  
   There are 8 dragon elements: Fire, Water, Ice, Wind, Plant, Earth, Energy, and Metal. Each primary element has 5 dragons. Higher-cost dragons have more element traits.

4. **High-pressure castle defense**  
   The castle wall has only 5 HP. Each enemy that crosses the far-left edge removes 1 HP. Letting 5 enemies through means defeat.

## Target Audience

- Players who enjoy Plants vs. Zombies, Kingdom Rush, Backpack Battles, and Teamfight Tactics.
- Short 10-20 minute sessions.
- Accessible on PC and mobile, with enough strategic depth for players who enjoy optimizing economy and positioning.

## Camera And Presentation

- 2D side/top hybrid view similar to PvZ, showing 5 horizontal lanes.
- The far-left side shows the castle wall.
- Dragons face right, monsters face left.
- The left 7 columns are the dragon placement zone.
- The right 2 columns are the monster approach zone.
- Dragon art uses Asset DML. Monster art uses Basic Demon, Basic Monster, Basic Undead, Toxic Sludge, Yeti, and Humanoid asset folders.

## Player Fantasy

The player is the Keeper of a dragon fortress. Monsters attack in waves, trying to break past the dragons and reach the castle wall. The player recruits dragons from the shop, combines duplicates into stronger stars, and builds a defensive formation before each wave.

## Core Loop

1. Enter preparation phase.
2. Use Gold to buy dragons from the 5-slot shop.
3. Reroll or lock the shop if needed.
4. Buy XP to increase Keeper Level and board cap.
5. Place dragons on the left 7 columns or hold them on the bench.
6. Merge 3 copies of the same dragon into a higher-star unit.
7. Start the wave and kill monsters to earn Gold.
8. Monsters that cross the castle wall remove 1 HP.
9. Repeat until the player clears all waves or Castle HP reaches 0.

## Win/Lose

- **Win:** Defeat the final wave or final boss.
- **Lose:** Castle HP reaches 0. The player starts with 5 HP, and each enemy that crosses the far-left castle wall removes 1 HP.

## USP

- PvZ-style lane defense plus TFT-style shop, bench, XP, and merging.
- 8 elements x 5 dragons per primary element, creating a 40-dragon drafting pool with multi-element higher-cost units.
- Tight 5-HP castle defense creates high pressure and clear stakes.
